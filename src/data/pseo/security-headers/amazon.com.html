The world's largest e-commerce platform processes hundreds of billions in annual transactions, handles payment data for hundreds of millions of customers, and stores sensitive purchase history. Despite this, Amazon deploys zero standard security headers on their main domain — a striking gap for a company of this scale and security exposure.

amazon.com implements 0 out of 10 recommended security headers. The average across our study of 85 top websites is 4.0/10.
