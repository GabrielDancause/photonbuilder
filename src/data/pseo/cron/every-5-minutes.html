When to Use This

The most common cron interval. Used for cache clearing, data syncing, API polling, queue workers, and monitoring checks.

Crontab Entry

*/5 * * * * */5 * * * * /usr/bin/php /var/www/app/artisan schedule:run >> /dev/null 2>&1
*/5 * * * * /path/to/your/script.sh

Things to Know

⚠️ Watch out

*/5 means at minutes 0, 5, 10, 15... not 'every 5 minutes from when the crontab was saved'. All */5 jobs across the system fire at the same times.

💡 Pro tip

If many jobs use */5, stagger them: one at */5, another at 1-56/5, another at 2-57/5. This prevents thundering herd problems.
