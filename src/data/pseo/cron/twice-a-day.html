When to Use This

Morning and evening digest emails, twice-daily data snapshots, AM/PM report generation.

Crontab Entry

0 9,21 * * * 0 9,21 * * * /opt/scripts/send-digest.sh
0 9,21 * * * /path/to/your/script.sh

Things to Know

⚠️ Watch out

The comma syntax (9,21) creates a list — fires at both specified hours. You can list as many hours as needed: 6,12,18 for three times a day.

💡 Pro tip

Choose times that bracket your users' active hours. 9 AM catches the morning crowd, 9 PM catches the evening reviewers.
