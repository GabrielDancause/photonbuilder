When to Use This

Business-hours tasks: daily standup notifications, morning report emails, opening-bell data fetches, Slack digest messages.

Crontab Entry

0 9 * * 1-5 0 9 * * 1-5 /opt/scripts/send-morning-report.sh
0 9 * * 1-5 /path/to/your/script.sh

Things to Know

⚠️ Watch out

1-5 means Monday(1) through Friday(5). Some systems use 0-6 where 0=Sunday, others use 1-7 where 1=Monday. Check your system's convention.

💡 Pro tip

For US business days, combine with month to skip holidays: you'll need a separate holiday calendar since cron doesn't understand holidays natively.
