When to Use This

Weekend-only maintenance, personal automation (home backup, photo sync), weekend digest emails.

Crontab Entry

0 10 * * 6,0 0 10 * * 6,0 /home/user/scripts/weekend-photo-backup.sh
0 10 * * 6,0 /path/to/your/script.sh

Things to Know

⚠️ Watch out

6,0 = Saturday and Sunday. Order doesn't matter. Some people write 0,6 — both work identically.

💡 Pro tip

For personal cron jobs on a home server, weekends are when you're likely around to check if something went wrong.
