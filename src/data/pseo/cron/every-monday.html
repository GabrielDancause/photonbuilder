When to Use This

Weekly reports, Monday morning summaries, sprint kickoff notifications, weekly data cleanup.

Crontab Entry

0 9 * * 1 0 9 * * 1 /opt/scripts/weekly-report.sh | mail -s 'Weekly Report' team@company.com
0 9 * * 1 /path/to/your/script.sh

Things to Know

⚠️ Watch out

Day 1 = Monday on most Linux systems (ISO standard). On some BSD/macOS systems, 1 might also mean Monday, but always verify with a test.

💡 Pro tip

For weekly tasks, Monday morning is ideal for forward-looking reports. For retrospectives, Friday afternoon (0 17 * * 5) is better.
