When to Use This

DNS propagation checks, SSL certificate monitoring, 4x-daily data warehouse loads, weather data fetching.

Crontab Entry

0 */6 * * * 0 */6 * * * certbot renew --quiet
0 */6 * * * /path/to/your/script.sh

Things to Know

⚠️ Watch out

Fires at midnight, 6 AM, noon, and 6 PM. If you need different times, specify them: '0 2,8,14,20 * * *'.

💡 Pro tip

Four times a day is enough for most monitoring and data syncing tasks. It's the most resource-friendly interval that still catches issues within half a business day.
