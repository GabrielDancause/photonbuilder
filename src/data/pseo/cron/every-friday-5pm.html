Things to Know

⚠️ Watch out

Hour 17 = 5 PM in 24-hour time. Cron always uses 24-hour format. 5 would mean 5 AM.

💡 Pro tip

Great for automated weekly summaries. Send team metrics and highlights so people can review over the weekend if they choose.
