When to Use This

Health checks, queue processing, real-time monitoring. Most aggressive standard interval — your cron daemon will fire this 1,440 times per day.

Crontab Entry

* * * * * flock -n /tmp/healthcheck.lock curl -s https://example.com/health > /dev/null
* * * * * /path/to/your/script.sh

Things to Know

⚠️ Watch out

Make sure your job completes in under 60 seconds, or you'll have overlapping executions. Use a lock file or flock command to prevent this.

💡 Pro tip

Consider every 5 minutes instead. Most monitoring systems don't need minute-level granularity, and you'll reduce server load by 80%.
