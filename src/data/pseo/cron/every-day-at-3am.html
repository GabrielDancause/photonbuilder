When to Use This

The classic 'off-peak maintenance window'. Database optimization, heavy backups, data warehouse ETL, full-text search reindexing.

Crontab Entry

0 3 * * * 0 3 * * * /opt/scripts/optimize-database.sh >> /var/log/db-optimize.log 2>&1
0 3 * * * /path/to/your/script.sh

Things to Know

⚠️ Watch out

3 AM is a popular choice for maintenance — your jobs might compete with the OS's own maintenance tasks (apt updates, log rotation). Stagger if needed.

💡 Pro tip

3 AM is ideal because it avoids both evening traffic and early morning report generation. Just make sure your task finishes before business hours start.
