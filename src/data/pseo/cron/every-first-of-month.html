When to Use This

Monthly billing runs, subscription renewals, monthly reports, certificate checks, storage cleanup.

Crontab Entry

0 0 1 * * 0 0 1 * * /opt/scripts/monthly-billing.sh >> /var/log/billing.log 2>&1
0 0 1 * * /path/to/your/script.sh

Things to Know

⚠️ Watch out

This fires on the 1st regardless of whether it's a weekday or weekend. If you need the first business day, you'll need a wrapper script that checks the day of week.

💡 Pro tip

For billing systems, consider running at 1 AM (0 1 1 * *) instead of midnight to account for any end-of-month processes that might still be running.
