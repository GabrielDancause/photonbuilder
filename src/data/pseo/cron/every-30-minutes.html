When to Use This

Report emails, database maintenance, log rotation checks, DNS record updates.

Crontab Entry

*/30 * * * * */30 * * * * /opt/scripts/send-digest.sh >> /var/log/digest.log 2>&1
*/30 * * * * /path/to/your/script.sh

Things to Know

⚠️ Watch out

This fires at :00 and :30 only. If your task takes longer than 30 minutes, the next execution will overlap.

💡 Pro tip

For tasks like sending digest emails, 30 minutes is often better than hourly — users get faster responses without the overhead of every-5-minutes processing.
