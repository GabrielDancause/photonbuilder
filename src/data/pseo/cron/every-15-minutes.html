When to Use This

Analytics processing, email queue flushing, sitemap regeneration, backup status checks. Fires 96 times per day.

Crontab Entry

*/15 * * * * */15 * * * * /usr/local/bin/generate-sitemap.sh
*/15 * * * * /path/to/your/script.sh

Things to Know

⚠️ Watch out

Only fires at :00, :15, :30, :45 — four times per hour. If you need more granularity, use */5 or */10.

💡 Pro tip

This is the sweet spot for most background tasks. Frequent enough to feel responsive, infrequent enough to not waste resources.
