When to Use This

Report generation, data aggregation, moderate-frequency polling. Fires 144 times per day.

Crontab Entry

*/10 * * * * */10 * * * * /opt/scripts/sync-data.sh >> /var/log/sync.log 2>&1
*/10 * * * * /path/to/your/script.sh

Things to Know

⚠️ Watch out

Like all step values, this fires at fixed times (:00, :10, :20, :30, :40, :50), not relative to when you set it up.

💡 Pro tip

Good balance between responsiveness and resource usage. If you don't need data fresher than 10 minutes, this is usually the right choice over */5.
