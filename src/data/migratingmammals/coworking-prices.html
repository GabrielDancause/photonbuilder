Methodology

This study collected data across over 50 cities popular with digital nomads. Pricing data was aggregated from multiple sources, primarily leveraging Nomadlist statistics for coworking hot desk costs. Day pass rates are estimated based on a standard calculation (approximately 10% of monthly cost with a $5 minimum) where direct day pass data was unavailable. Internet speeds represent the average reported speeds for the city, and the count of spaces is an indicator of coworking availability.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which digital nomad city has the cheapest coworking spaces?
Based on our 2026 data, Buenos Aires offers some of the lowest coworking prices at roughly $66 per month for a hot desk. Other very affordable locations include cities in Southeast Asia and South America.
How much does a coworking day pass usually cost?
A coworking day pass typically ranges from $10 to $30 USD depending on the city. In expensive hubs like London, New York, or Lisbon, you might pay $25-$35 per day. In more affordable hubs like Chiang Mai or Medellin, a day pass is closer to $10-$20.
Is it cheaper to get a monthly hot desk or pay for day passes?
If you plan to work from a coworking space more than 8-10 times a month, a monthly hot desk membership is almost always cheaper. Monthly memberships often come with added perks like 24/7 access, lockers, and free coffee.
Do all coworking spaces have fast internet?
Not always. While coworking spaces generally invest in better internet infrastructure than local cafes, the speeds are still limited by the city's overall infrastructure. Our data shows cities like Lisbon have excellent speeds (38+ Mbps avg), while places like Medellin might be slower (9 Mbps avg).
Can I negotiate coworking space prices?
Yes, especially in locations with a high density of coworking spaces or during off-peak seasons. You can often negotiate discounts if you commit to multiple months or sign up as a group. However, large corporate spaces like WeWork rarely negotiate on individual hot desk prices.
