Best Free Website Builders 2026

Compare the top freemium website building platforms. We've analyzed the real features, limitations, and use cases for the best free plans available this year.

1. Wix

Best for Beginners
Free Subdomain
yoursite.wixsite.com
Storage limit
500 MB
Bandwidth
500 MB

Wix provides an intuitive, drag-and-drop editor that makes it incredibly easy for anyone to create a website without coding. Their free plan gives you access to hundreds of templates and basic features. However, it does include Wix branding at the top of your site and limits you to a Wix subdomain.

2. Webflow

Best for Designers
Free Subdomain
yoursite.webflow.io
CMS Items
50 items
Bandwidth
1 GB

Webflow bridges the gap between design and development by generating clean HTML, CSS, and JavaScript as you build visually. The free "Starter" plan is excellent for learning the platform and building small projects or portfolios, allowing up to 2 pages and 50 CMS items, though it requires a Webflow badge.

3. WordPress.com

Best for Blogging
Free Subdomain
yoursite.wordpress.com
Storage limit
1 GB
Plugins/Themes
Limited (Free only)

Powered by Automattic, WordPress.com offers a hosted version of the popular WordPress software. The free tier is robust for writers and bloggers, offering excellent out-of-the-box SEO. You cannot install third-party plugins or premium themes on the free plan, and it displays WordPress.com ads.

4. Framer

Best for Prototypes
Free Subdomain
yoursite.framer.website
Visitors
1,000 / month
Pages
1 page (Free)

Framer is a modern website builder that feels like a design tool (similar to Figma). It's exceptional for building fast, highly interactive sites with complex animations. The free plan is limited to a single page and a Framer-branded domain, making it ideal for landing pages and simple portfolios.

5. Weebly

Best for Simple E-commerce
Free Subdomain
yoursite.square.site
Storage limit
500 MB
E-commerce
Basic (Square integration)

Now owned by Square, Weebly focuses heavily on simplicity and integrated e-commerce. It remains one of the few builders that allows you to sell items on a completely free plan, though you will be subject to transaction fees and Weebly branding on your live site.
