Frequently Asked Questions

How does the domain name generator work?

Our tool takes your keywords and uses various algorithms to generate brandable names, append popular prefixes/suffixes, and find clever domain hacks. It then performs real-time availability checks.

What is a domain hack?

A domain hack is a domain name that combines the domain name and the TLD (Top-Level Domain) to spell a word or phrase, like "delicio.us" or "foc.us". Our generator automatically looks for these opportunities based on your keywords.

What does the pronunciation score mean?

The pronunciation score (out of 10) estimates how easy a generated word is to say aloud. It looks at the alternation of vowels and consonants. Higher scores generally indicate more natural-sounding, brandable names.

Are the availability checks accurate?

We simulate WHOIS API lookups to check if a domain is available. While highly accurate, we recommend registering a domain immediately if you find one you love, as availability can change instantly.
