Frequently Asked Questions

What is a favicon?
A favicon (short for favorite icon) is a small image displayed next to your website's title in the browser tab, bookmarks, and history. It helps users easily identify your site visually.
What sizes are required for modern favicons?
Modern websites typically need a few standard sizes: 16x16 and 32x32 for desktop browsers, 180x180 for Apple touch icons (iOS), and 192x192 for Android home screens. Our tool generates all these sizes automatically.
Can I use an SVG file for my favicon?
Yes! Modern browsers fully support SVG favicons, which remain perfectly sharp on all screen resolutions and can even adapt to light and dark modes. If you upload an SVG, our tool will provide it along with standard PNG fallbacks for older browsers.
How do I add the generated favicons to my website?
After downloading the ZIP file, extract it and upload the icon files to the root directory of your website. Then, copy the provided HTML meta tags from our tool and paste them inside the <head> section of your website's HTML source code.
Why do I need an apple-touch-icon.png?
Apple devices use the apple-touch-icon.png file when a user saves your website to their home screen as a shortcut. Providing this specific 180x180 pixel image ensures your site's icon looks perfect and appropriately sized on iPhones and iPads.
