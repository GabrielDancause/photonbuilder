Why Use a Multi-Timer?

Timing is everything in cooking. Whether you're preparing a holiday feast with multiple dishes finishing at different times or just trying to coordinate pasta and sauce, a single kitchen timer rarely cuts it. Our multi-timer allows you to track every component of your meal simultaneously.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will the timer ring if my phone screen turns off?
For the best experience, keep your browser open and the screen on. Most modern mobile browsers pause JavaScript execution when the screen is off or the app is in the background, which may delay the alarm until you wake the device.

How do I stop the alarm?
When a timer reaches zero, the card will turn pink, pulse, and an alarm will sound. Click the "Stop" button on the timer card to silence it and clear the timer.
