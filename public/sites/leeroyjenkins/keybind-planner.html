Why use a Keybind Layout Planner?

A structured keybind layout is the foundation of mechanical skill in PC gaming. Whether you are learning a new complex MMO or trying to optimize your mechanics for competitive shooters, visualizing your keybinds helps build muscle memory faster. This tool allows you to visually group abilities by color—such as placing all movement abilities on blue keys and defensive cooldowns on green keys—ensuring your hand placement is logical and efficient.

Popular Gaming Keybind Templates

  • FPS (First-Person Shooter): Centralized around WASD for movement. Key actions like reload (R), use (E), and crouch (Ctrl/C) are kept immediately adjacent to the movement cluster to minimize the time your fingers spend away from strafe keys.
  • MOBA (Multiplayer Online Battle Arena): Anchored on QWER for champion abilities. Summoner spells (D/F) and active items (1-6) are positioned for quick access with the index and middle fingers, prioritizing reaction speed over complex movement combinations.
  • MMO (Massively Multiplayer Online): Highly complex layouts that utilize modifier keys (Shift, Ctrl, Alt) combined with numbers (1-0) and surrounding letters (Q, E, R, F, C, V) to map dozens of spells and macros within easy reach of the left hand.

How to export and share your layout

Once you have perfected your custom keybind layout, you can easily save it. Click the Generate JSON button to create a text representation of your setup. You can copy this text and save it to a document for safekeeping, or share it with friends. To load a layout later, simply paste the JSON text back into the box and click Import JSON.
