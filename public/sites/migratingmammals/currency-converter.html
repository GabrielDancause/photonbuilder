Offline Currency Conversion

Our currency converter tool operates completely offline after the initial page load. All computation runs client-side directly within your browser. This means you can bookmark this page and use it even when you don't have internet access—a perfect solution for traveling in remote locations or during flights.

Supported Currencies

We support over 50 of the world's most traded fiat currencies, as well as several major cryptocurrencies. Whether you need to calculate expenses for an upcoming trip or analyze cross-border payments, our tool provides a comprehensive selection:

  • Major Fiat: USD (US Dollar), EUR (Euro), GBP (British Pound), JPY (Japanese Yen), CAD (Canadian Dollar), AUD (Australian Dollar), and more.
  • Global Fiat: AED, BRL, CNY, INR, MXN, SGD, ZAR, and dozens of other global currencies.
  • Cryptocurrencies: Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Solana (SOL), and others.

Privacy & Security

Because this tool does not rely on external API calls for each conversion, your financial queries remain strictly on your own device. No data regarding the amounts you enter or the currencies you select is ever transmitted to or stored on our servers.
