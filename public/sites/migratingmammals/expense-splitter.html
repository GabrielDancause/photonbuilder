How the Trip Expense Splitter Works

Managing finances during a group trip can quickly become complicated, especially when multiple people are paying for different things. Our Trip Expense Splitter simplifies this process by keeping a running tally of who paid for what and automatically calculating the most efficient way to settle debts at the end of the trip.

Minimum Transaction Algorithm

Nobody wants to make five different bank transfers to settle their trip debts. This tool uses a "minimum transaction" algorithm that reduces the total number of payments needed. Instead of paying back every individual person you owe, the algorithm calculates everyone's net balance and matches those who owe money directly with those who are owed money, ensuring the minimum possible number of transfers.

Handling Multiple Currencies

When traveling internationally, you might pay for flights in your home currency, hotels in another, and daily expenses in local cash. This tool allows you to set a base currency and add custom exchange rates for any other currencies you use. All expenses are automatically converted back to your base currency for the final settlement calculation, meaning you never have to do manual conversions again.

  • Privacy First: All calculations are done entirely in your browser. No financial data or names are sent to our servers.
  • Works Offline: Once the page is loaded, you can continue to add expenses and calculate settlements even if you lose your internet connection.
  • Easy Sharing: Use the copy button to quickly share the final settlement list with your travel group via WhatsApp, iMessage, or email.
