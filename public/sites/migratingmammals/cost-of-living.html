Why Compare Cost of Living?

When considering a move to a new city or country as a digital nomad or expat, the cost of living is one of the most critical factors. Understanding how housing, daily food expenses, local transport, and utilities differ from your current location allows you to budget effectively and determine the true value of your income in a new context.

Core Expenses to Consider

  • Rent: Usually the largest monthly expense. This can vary wildly depending on proximity to the city center and the size of the apartment.
  • Food & Groceries: Includes everyday staples from local supermarkets, markets, and occasional dining out.
  • Transport: Costs for monthly public transit passes, gas prices, or ridesharing services.
  • Utilities: Essential home services including electricity, heating, water, and high-speed internet required for remote work.

How This Calculator Works

This comparator tool calculates the total estimated monthly cost for two different cities based on your inputs. It then provides the absolute difference and the percentage difference, giving you a clear picture of how much more or less expensive your target city is compared to your baseline. All calculations are performed directly in your browser, ensuring your data remains private and the tool works instantly, even offline.
