How to Use the Tip Calculator

Our free tip calculator makes it easy to quickly figure out how much you should leave for a tip and what your final bill will be. It's especially useful when dining out with a group and needing to split the bill evenly.

Simply enter your total bill amount before tip, choose a standard tip percentage or enter your own custom amount, and indicate how many people are splitting the bill. If you select the "Round Up Total Bill" option, the calculator will automatically adjust the total tip to ensure your final bill amount is a clean, whole dollar amount, making it even easier to split.

Standard Tipping Etiquette

  • 15% to 20%: The standard tipping range for sit-down restaurants with good service. 18% is often considered the baseline for standard service, while 20% or more is for excellent service.
  • 10% to 15%: For buffet restaurants or situations where the server does less active table service.
  • 10% or less: Generally reserved for poor service, though communicating issues with management is often preferred.
  • Takeout / Coffee Shops: Tipping is generally optional, but 10% or a few dollars in the tip jar is appreciated for large or complex orders.
