🚨 Key Insight

Very few jobs specifically mention AI/generative tools in their requirements. The opportunity is to BRING AI skills to traditional design roles. Employers don't yet know to ask for these skills, but candidates who demonstrate AI proficiency in their portfolio stand out massively.
