🚨 Salary Insights

The median salary for a mid-level remote graphic designer in Canadian e-commerce is approximately $72,000 CAD. Roles that explicitly require motion design (After Effects), video editing, or AI workflow integration generally command a 15-20% premium.

🔍 How to Search for These Roles

Landing a remote design role in e-commerce requires a specific approach compared to agency or traditional corporate design jobs.

Performance Show Metrics

E-commerce cares about conversion. If an ad you designed increased CTR by 20%, state that in your portfolio.

Volume Show Systems

Demonstrate how you use templates, Figma components, or AI to produce assets efficiently at scale.

Motion Show Video

Static images aren't enough for modern DTC brands. Short-form video and basic animation are required.

Targeted Search Strategy

  1. Skip general job boards: Focus directly on the careers pages of growing Shopify merchants. Use directories like Store Leads to filter top Canadian Shopify stores.
  2. Search terms matter: Instead of just "Graphic Designer," search for "Digital Designer," "Performance Creative," "Growth Designer," or "Brand Designer."
  3. LinkedIn Networking: Connect with Creative Directors or Growth Marketing Managers at Canadian DTC brands. They are often the hiring managers for these roles.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is the average salary for a remote e-commerce graphic designer in Canada?

Based on our research for 2025, mid-level remote e-commerce graphic designers in Canada earn between $60,000 and $85,000 CAD, while senior roles can reach up to $105,000 CAD depending on the company size and specific requirements like motion design or 3D.

Do Canadian e-commerce companies hire fully remote designers?

Yes, many prominent Canadian e-commerce brands like Shopify, SSENSE, Article, and Knix offer fully remote or remote-first positions for graphic designers. However, they may require you to be based in Canada for tax and payroll purposes.

What portfolio elements are crucial for e-commerce design roles?

E-commerce companies look for high-conversion design elements. Your portfolio must include performance marketing creatives (social media ads, email campaigns), cohesive brand system implementation, landing page designs, and ideally, some motion graphics.

Are AI skills required for e-commerce graphic design jobs in 2025?

While not always explicitly listed, proficiency with AI tools like Adobe Firefly, Midjourney, or DALL-E for rapid asset generation and variant creation is becoming a major competitive advantage in fast-paced e-commerce environments.
