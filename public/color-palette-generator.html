Frequently Asked Questions

What is a WCAG contrast ratio?

The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) define contrast ratios to ensure text is readable for users with visual impairments. A ratio of 4.5:1 is required for normal text (AA level), while 7:1 is preferred (AAA level). For large text, 3:1 is acceptable.

How do the harmony rules work?

Harmony rules use color theory to generate mathematically pleasing palettes based on a base color on the color wheel. Analogous colors are next to each other, Complementary are opposites, and Triadic forms an equilateral triangle.

How does the Brand Extractor work?

The brand extractor analyzes the CSS and images of a provided URL to determine the most frequently used and dominant colors, creating a cohesive palette that represents the brand's identity.
